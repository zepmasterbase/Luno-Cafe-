Our Story

Randfontein’s premium student café experience

Luno Café is located in Randfontein, Gauteng, South Africa — built as a modern sanctuary for students, creators, and dreamers who need a space to think, work, and connect.

We combine luxury café aesthetics with authentic African student culture, creating a space that feels both premium and familiar.

At Luno Café, every cup is crafted to fuel ambition — whether you're studying, building ideas, or meeting friends.
